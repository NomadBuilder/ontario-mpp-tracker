How to use this
  1. Find your MPP — search by name or riding, or enter your postal code and press Enter.
  2. Campaign filters — narrow the list by Yes / No / No Show on individual featured bills.
  3. Expense spotlight — chip filters for top spenders, $50k/$100k+, hospitality-heavy, travel-heavy, and above/below party median. Sort by total, hospitality, or travel.
  4. Take action — email or call from the card, or open the OLA expense disclosure link.
