How to use this

  1. Find your MPP — search by name or riding, or enter your postal code and press Enter.
  2. Campaign filters — show only MPPs who voted a certain way on key bills (e.g. Yes on Bill 5 and Bill 97).
  3. Open a card — party and voting history at a glance.
  4. Take action — email or call from the card, or switch to the full data table.
