Why this exists

Accountability shouldn’t require a spreadsheet

Ontario’s Legislative Assembly publishes votes, expense filings, and contact details — but they’re scattered across sites most people never open. OAC pulls that public record into one place so you can act on it.

  • Votes on bills that reshape Ontario Land use, transparency, airports, privatization — see Yes / No / No Show by name.
  • Expense disclosure, in plain language Travel, meals, hospitality, and more from ola.org — not hidden in PDFs.
  • A direct line to your representative Email or call from the page. Pressure works when it’s personal and public.

Disclosed travel, accommodation, meals, and hospitality across Ontario’s MPPs over roughly two years — from the Legislative Assembly’s public filings.
